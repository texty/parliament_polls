Ukrainian parliamentary election 2019

(«Poll of polls» model)

We took all reliable polls and plotted aggregated voter's intentions on interactive chart. The values are percentage among .
Last updated: July 9, 2019

Click on bars with name of party to add or remove corresponding charts.

Click on bars with name of party to add or remove corresponding charts.

Each line on chart is an evolution of median for support rate for some political party. We calculated it using probabilistic (bayesian) model.

Take into account that support level for some political party in form of single number is an oversimplification. We've got a whole distribution for level of support, so besides median you should look to entire range of values for specific date.

Pay an attention to the band of color around each line ― this is the range of values for level of support for specific party. With a high probability (0.95), the level of support is within such band.

Please, remember: this is NOT an election forecast! This is just aggregated levels of support for political parties based on data from different polls.
